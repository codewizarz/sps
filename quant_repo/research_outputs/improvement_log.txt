PROBLEM STATEMENT

Meow Final Boss (V3/Iter2) validation revealed:

ROOT CAUSE ANALYSIS
-------------------
1. STOP-LOSS COOLDOWN OVER-SUPPRESSION
   - Stop-loss on 2026-01-30 triggered cooldown = 1 cycle
   - This blocked ALL entries for the next expiry cycle (~1 week)
   - With only weekly cycles in 2026, this created 2+ week dead zones
   Fix: V4-C frequency stabilizer overrides cooldown when 0 trades in
        last 28 days, restoring entry eligibility immediately.

2. STATIC OI/VOLUME QUALITY GATES TOO STRICT
   - 2026 data environment: lower OI and volume than 2025
   - Hard-blocking on OI < 75K or Vol < 75K rejected valid entries
   Fix: V4-B soft quality filtering — OI/volume shortfalls produce a
        size penalty (55-65% of normal size) instead of hard rejection.
        Only zero-premium and extreme spreads (>3x threshold) still hard-block.

3. 100% LOW VOL REGIME BIAS
   - All 51 V3 trades were in LOW VOL regime
   - Strategy was not generalizing across vol environments
   Fix: V4-A adaptive percentile thresholds replace static 10%/20% cutoffs.
        Rolling 252-day P30/P70 of RV20 determine regime boundaries.
        This makes the strategy regime-relative, not absolute-threshold bound.

4. DISCRETE REGIME CLIFF-EDGE SIZING
   - Crossing 10% or 20% RV boundary caused abrupt position sizing jumps
   - Created instability at boundary conditions
   Fix: V4-D continuous sigmoid scaling function: smoothly scales from
        ceiling=1.05 (deep low vol) to floor=0.45 (high vol), no cliff edges.

5. LOW VOL OVER-ALLOCATION
   - max_position_fraction=1.15 in LOW VOL → large positions → large stop losses
   - Single large loss disproportionately impacts P&L
   Fix: V4-E low_vol_max_position_fraction=0.95 caps LOW VOL sizing.
        Also added progressive drawdown de-risking: if DD > 5%, each additional
        1% DD further reduces position size by 12%, floored at 45%.
